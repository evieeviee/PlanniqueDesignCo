Frequently Asked Questions

Everything to know before starting

Clear answers about content, domains, revisions, maintenance and project delivery.

Do I need to buy a domain?

Yes. The business should own its domain name. Plannique can help check availability and connect it to the completed website.

What content do I need to provide?

Please provide the logo, business introduction, service information, prices, photographs, contact details, address and social media links.

Will the website work on mobile phones?

Yes. Every package includes responsive design for mobile, tablet and desktop screens.

Can I update the website later?

Yes. Future content changes and maintenance can be quoted separately.

Are hosting and domain fees included?

Paid domain registration, hosting subscriptions and third-party systems are separate unless specifically included in the quotation.

Can a demo design be customised?

Yes. The layout can be adapted using the client’s brand name, logo, colours, content, images and preferred functions.

How do revisions work?

Revision rounds depend on the selected package. Clients should submit consolidated feedback for each round.

How quickly will the website be ready?

Delivery depends on the selected package and how quickly complete content and feedback are provided.
